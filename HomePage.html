About Me

My name is Gabriel Toro, currently enrolled at Mount Royal University, who moved to Canada at a very young age. I have plans to graduate with a major in Computer Information Systems, and change the technology world with my creative ideas.


Using my extensive knowledge of video games and music, I plan on creating a game, directed towards adults, that allows them to temporarily become the kid they used to be. Many people believe the process of becoming an adult is to let go of your life as a child, but I disagree. Sure, you have to pay taxes, a mortgage, insurance, find a stable job that allows you to live comfortably. But for what reason? My reason is so I can do the things I love the most: Listening to my favorite music, immersing myself in different worlds through games, and helping the society. Therefore my dream to create a childhood nostalgia, leads me towards a happier life, and perhaps it could do the same for others. Using my degree in Computer Information Systems, I hope to achieve that.
